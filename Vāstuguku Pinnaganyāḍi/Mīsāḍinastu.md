Mīsāḍinastu (Misadinasthu) is a city located on the [[Isle of Pranta]] in -------, populated primarily by Mīsāḍinukvalnu (Eng: Misadinites). It is a coastal city, and as such, Misadinasthu has been a predominant naval power for much of its history. It is currently the capital of the Kingdom of Misadinasthu.
