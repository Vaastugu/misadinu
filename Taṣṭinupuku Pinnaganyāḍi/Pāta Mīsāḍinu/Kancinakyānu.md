# Etymology
[[Taṣṭinupuku Pinnaganyāḍi/Pāta Mīsāḍinu/kanci|kanci]] (sun) + [[nākku]] (-like) + [[Yānu]] (noun marker)

Use of [[Taṣṭinupuku Pinnaganyāḍi/Pāta Mīsāḍinu/kanci|kanci]] possibly due to association with [[Taṣṭinupuku Pinnaganyāḍi/Pāta Mīsāḍinu/sōkanci|sōkanci]], which can be translated to 'Shadow/night guardian' in religious or literary contexts. So, by extension, [[Taṣṭinupuku Pinnaganyāḍi/Pāta Mīsāḍinu/kanci|kanci]] came to mean possibly 'day guardian'. Thus, a guard is 'sun-like'.

## Noun
1. Protection
2. Guard (unit)
