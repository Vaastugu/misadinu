## Etymology
Literally "the darkness from a deep sea"
Not related to [[Taṣṭinupuku Pinnaganyāḍi/Pāta Mīsāḍinu/gōlamu|gōlamu]] ([[Pāta Mīsāḍinu]])
### Noun
1. Abyss
2. Vast empty space
3. [[The Formless Expanse]] (religious)
