
## Etymology
*Ultimately from [[Proto-Prankita]]*. 
*Cognates*: [[Yāṣva (Yā)]] ([[Sōmyamu Mīsāḍinu (Language)|S. Mīsāḍinu]], that), [[Yōṣva (Yō)]] ([[Sōmyamu Mīsāḍinu (Language)|S. Mīsāḍinu]], "which?")

### Noun
1. This.

Accusative: au -> o, aa -> ā, oo -> ō.
