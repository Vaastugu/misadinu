## Etymology
*From [[cāmanttu]] (tool, [[Pāta Mīsāḍinu]])*.
### Noun
1. Tool.

### Verb
1. To wield (a weapon or tool) - [[Camattyākina]]

#### Declension (Type-B)

Accusative: au -> o, aa -> ā, oo -> ō.
