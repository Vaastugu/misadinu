## Etymology
*Cognates with* [[Bāsti]] (egg) ([[Sōmyamu Mīsāḍinu (Language)|S. Mīsāḍinu]])

### Noun
1. Seed (of a plant or tree)
2. Source
		See also: [[ōmūla]] (origin)
3. Beginning (of a journey) (metaphorical, literary)
		*Coordinate term*: [[Cōpu]] (tree, destination)
4. Tears (RELIGIOUS ONLY) - specifically the tears of [[Vāstuguku Pinnaganyāḍi/Dūramupu/Mīsāḍinagyāra Dūramu/Dārgapē (The Gods).md|Astadārguṣa]].
